1, select the first name and last name of employees who are in IT department

Answer:
select first_name,last_name ,department_name from  employee as e inner join department as d on (e.department_id = d.department_id)where department_name="IT department";

2. select first name, last name, department name and salary of all the employees who has a salary that is NOT lower than 70000 in the IT department.

Answer:
select first_name,last_name ,department_name from  employee as e inner join department as d on (e.department_id = d.department_id)where department_name="IT department" and salary>=70000;
